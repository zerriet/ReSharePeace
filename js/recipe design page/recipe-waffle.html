Homemade Waffle
By Jamie

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavoured Italian dessert.
It is made of ladyfingers (savoiardi) dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa.

Ingredients

  1. Eggs yolks
  2. Sugar
  3. Milk
  4. Heavy Cream
  5. Vanilla Extract
  6. Cheese
  7. Coffe
  8. Rum
  9. Cookies
  10. Cocoa Powder

Directions

  • Step 1
    Whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils
  • Step 2
    Beat cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form
  • Step 3
    Whisk mascarpone into yolk mixture until smooth
  • Step 4
    Combine coffee and rum
